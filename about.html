About Baker T

Our Story - From Passion to Pastry

Baker T began as a dream in 2015 when our founder, Thomas Baker, decided to share his grandmother's secret recipes with the world. What started as a small home bakery has blossomed into the beloved neighborhood pastry shop you see today.

The Art of Baking

Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to begin the delicate process of creating our signature pastries. We use only the finest ingredients - French butter, Belgian chocolate, organic flour, and locally-sourced fruits. Each pastry is handcrafted with attention to detail and a commitment to excellence.

Our Philosophy

At Baker T, we believe that a great pastry can brighten anyone's day. Whether it's a morning croissant with coffee, a birthday cake for a special celebration, or simply a sweet treat to share with friends, we put love and care into everything we bake.

Community & Sustainability

We're proud to be part of this community. We source ingredients locally when possible, donate day-old pastries to local shelters, and use eco-friendly packaging. Our goal is to make the world a little sweeter, one pastry at a time.

Meet Our Head Baker

Ayaka Tanaka

With over 15 years of experience and training from Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Ayaka brings authentic French pastry techniques to every creation. Her passion for baking started in her grandmother's kitchen and has evolved into an art form that delights customers daily.

"Baking is not just about following recipes - it's about creating moments of joy. Every pastry tells a story, and I want ours to be memorable."

Our Amazing Team

Behind every delicious pastry is a dedicated team of bakers, decorators, and baristas who share our passion for excellence. Together, we work to ensure that every visit to Baker T is special.
